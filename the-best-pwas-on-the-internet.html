The best PWAs on the Internet

Progressive Web Apps is Google's push at unifying the web, so to speak. They are simple websites, under the PWA umbrella, with native app like functions. For example, there is the auto dark/light mode based on user' preferences set on the OS level, maskable icons and push notifications. Upcoming features include app shortcuts (Chrome 84-85) like those on Android and running at device startup.
