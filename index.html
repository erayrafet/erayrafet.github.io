    Black Pixel browser themes

    Black Pixel is a theme made especially for Google Chrome, but supports Microsoft's new Chromium-based Edge browser as well. It combines the almighty black color with white text/icons and my favorite #1a73e8 blue color (used throughout this website and all my other projects) from Google's past iterations of their Material Design Color Palette.
    The Mozilla Firefox edition carries over the same color palette, but with a few enhancements to accommodate the differences in UI layout.
