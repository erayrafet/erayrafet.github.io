    Black Pixel browser themes

    Black Pixel is a theme made especially for Google Chrome, but supports Microsoft's new Chromium-based Edge browser as well. It combines the almighty black color with white text/icons and my favorite #1a73e8 blue color (used across this website) from Google's past iterations of their Material Design Color Palette.
    The Mozilla Firefox edition carries over the same color palette, but with a few enhancements to accommodate the differences in UI layout.

    • Black Pixel for Google Chrome and Chrome OS
    • Black Pixel for Microsoft Edge
    • Black Pixel for Mozilla Firefox
    Black Pixel theme for Telegram's official Android and Desktop apps

    With Black Pixel for Telegram, my one and only goal is to keep the same minimal UI language style I've grown to love because of Google - absent of dividers and distractions, with vibrant colors here and there, whilst making it easier to focus on the important things first and foremost.
